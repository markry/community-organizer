# {{app_name}} — Member Guide

## Signing In

Visit the site and sign in using one of these methods:
- **Sign in with Google** — click the blue button. No password needed if you are using a browser window that is authenticated to Google (if not, you know how to do that :-). Works with any Google account (Gmail, Google Workspace, *etc.*).
- **Email and password** — enter the email address your admin registered, and your password.

**New user, or forgot your password?** On the sign-in page, click **New user or forgot your password?**. Enter your email address and the system will send you a verification code (if your email is in the system). Enter the code on the next page and choose a password. This same flow handles both first-time setup and password reset — the system never creates a temporary password on your behalf.

Passwords must be at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and a number.

You'll stay signed in on the device you used for 30 days. After that, just sign in again. You can sign in on as many devices as you like.

## Notification Settings

We recommend configuring your reminder preferences right after your first sign-in. Click **Your notification settings** at the bottom of your home page.

Choose when you'd like to receive reminder emails before each {{event}}:
- 1 day before
- 12 hours before
- 2 hours before
- 1 hour before
- 30 minutes before

You can select multiple options or none. Changes take effect immediately for all future {{events}}.

## Your Home Page

After signing in, you'll see down the page:
- **Pending trades** — if you have any open trade requests, they appear in a yellow box showing both sides of the proposed trade and whether it's a hard drop or you're keeping your slot.
- **Your upcoming {{events}}** — a list of {{events}} you're assigned to, showing the date, time, and {{event}} name. Each {{event}} has a **Trade** button and a **Withdraw** button.
- **View entire schedule and add yourself to slots** — see all upcoming {{events}} for all {{volunteers}} in {{app_name}}.
- **Your notification settings** — control your future reminder emails.
- **Sign out**

## Viewing the Schedule

Click **View entire schedule and add yourself to slots** to see not only your assignments but the published schedule for your entire group. Only published months are visible — if your admin hasn't published a month yet, it won't appear.

For each {{event}} you'll see:
- The date and time.
- The {{event}} name.
- Who is currently assigned.
- Whether and where there are open slots you could sign up for.
  - The table shows the minimum and desired number in the **Filled** column with a format like 1/2. That means only one of two desired {{volunteers}} is currently assigned. If you are taking additional slots, ideally fill in those with less than the desired number.
- You can also sign up as an additional {{volunteer}} even if the desired number has already signed up. This will be limited by some maximum per {{event}} set by your administrator.

You can also click **Send schedule to me by email** on the schedule page or your home page to receive the complete schedule table by email. This is useful for printing or having a quick reference in your inbox.

## Signing Up for a Slot

On the schedule page, if any {{event}} has open slots, you'll see a **Sign me up** link next to the {{volunteer}} names. Click it to sign up for that {{event}}. The page will update and your name will show next to the {{event}}.

You'll receive a confirmation email with the details, including the arrival time. The email includes a calendar invitation that you can accept to add the {{event}} to your calendar, or reject to inform the system that you can't take the assignment (more on that below).

## Withdrawing from a Slot

If you can no longer attend {{events}} you signed up for:

1. On your home page or the schedule page, find the {{event}}.
2. Click **withdraw** next to your name. On your home page, use the **Withdraw** button.
3. You'll receive a confirmation email. A calendar cancellation is included to remove the {{event}} from your calendar.

**You can also withdraw by declining the calendar invitation** directly from your email or calendar app. The system will automatically remove your assignment and send you a confirmation.

**What happens when you withdraw from a slot:**
- If the {{event}} still has full coverage (enough other {{volunteers}}), only you are notified.
- If coverage drops below the desired level:
  - Other {{volunteers}} already covering the same {{event}} are notified of the change.
  - Members of your **cohort** — people who typically volunteer at the same time as you — are notified that a slot opened up and asked to sign up.
  - Your Application Admin is notified of the coverage drop.

**What is a cohort?** A cohort is a group of {{volunteers}} who typically cover the same time slot (for example, "Sunday 8:00 AM regulars"). Your admin assigns you to one or more cohorts. When someone in your cohort releases a slot, you'll receive an email asking if you can cover. This way, the most relevant people hear about openings first. If you are not part of a cohort, you will not be automatically notified of any changes (although your admin might reach out to you for help).

**Please also consider trading** before withdrawing entirely. If you can volunteer on a different day, visit the schedule page and use the **Trade** button to request a swap. This helps maintain coverage while still finding a time that works for you.

## Trading Slots

If you can't make your assigned {{event}} but could volunteer at a different time:

1. Click the **Trade** button next to your assignment (on the home page or schedule page).
2. Choose your trade mode:
   - **Release slot immediately** (hard drop) — your slot opens up right away and you offer to trade for a different one.
   - **Keep slot while looking** (soft) — you hold your current slot until someone accepts the trade.
3. Select which slot(s) you'd prefer in exchange.
4. Click **Request trade**.

Other {{volunteers}} who can fill your slot will be notified. When someone accepts, you'll both receive confirmation emails with updated calendar invitations for your new assignments.

You can cancel a pending trade from your home page at any time.

## Calendar Integration

When you are assigned to an {{event}}, you'll receive a calendar invitation attached to the email. You can:

- **Accept** — adds the {{event}} to your calendar. Recommended.
- **Decline** — this automatically withdraws you from the slot (same as clicking Withdraw on the website). You'll receive a confirmation email.
- **Tentative** — our system does not support tentative responses. You'll receive an email asking you to accept or decline, with a new calendar invitation attached. If you'd like to trade for a different slot, please accept first, then visit the website to request a trade. Trades cannot be accomplished by email.

Calendar events are automatically added when you sign up and removed when you withdraw.

## Emails You'll Receive

| Email | When you get it |
|---|---|
| **Schedule published** | When the admin publishes a new month — lists your assignments with calendar invitations. |
| **You've signed up** / **You're assigned** | When you sign up for {{events}} (or an admin assigns you). Includes calendar invitation. |
| **Reminders** | Before each {{event}}, based on your notification settings. |
| **Withdrawal confirmation** | Confirmation after you withdraw. Includes calendar cancellation. |
| **Withdrawn via calendar** | Confirmation after you decline a calendar invitation. |
| **Cohort opening** | When someone at your usual time releases a slot and coverage is needed. |
| **Cohort coverage update** | When someone assigned to the same {{event}} as you releases their slot. |
| **Trade completed** | When a trade you requested or accepted is completed. Includes calendar invitation for your new slot. |

All emails come from `{{from_address}}` and include a link to the schedule where you can take action. Admin-initiated emails show the admin's name in the From field (*e.g.*, "John Doe of Rapids Swim Club"). You can reply to any scheduling email — your reply will be forwarded to the administrators.

## Signing Out

Click **Sign out** at the bottom of any page. You'll be returned to the sign-in page. Beware that closing your browser won't sign you out. You'll stay signed in for 30 days or until you explicitly choose **Sign out** from a signed-in browser window.
